So what is rpsxo?

rpsxo is shorthand for Rock Paper Scissors Tic Tac Toe.

So what does that mean exactly? Simple! Just regular tic tac toe,

but instead of placing X's and O's, you're placing rock, paper, scissors.

The goal is still the same: get three of the same thing in a line.

However, now you can change items on the grid, with RPS rules (R>S, P>R, S>P)

Also, ownership is disregarded, meaning that any tile can be used by any player for a win.

Is this really that complicated?

Short answer: yes. Slightly less short answer: the number of possible board paths does not fit in a js array.

Once you play the game, you discover how that the 3x3 grid provides ridiculously complicated challenges.

seriously, for such a small game, you will not believe how strategic it can get.
